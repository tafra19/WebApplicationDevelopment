Project Idea

Choose My Meal

User problem: Not being able to pick a meal to cook at home. This website will prompt users to answer a couple of questions to take their preferences/meal restrictions into account, then randomly generate a meal and provide the recipe for it.

User: People who are indecisive like me, are feeling a little adventerous, but don't want to spend money on eating out.

Comparison: Users can just directly search up recipes on a search engine, but this website will add a little fun to the process of choosing what to cook.

Expansion Idea: Users can contribute to the recipe database
